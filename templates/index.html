"I love
Sounding Different"

RichArt MusiQ born Richard Chounoune fell in love with music when his mother gifted him a guitar for his 15th birthday. Since then, RichArt MusiQ not only taught himself the guitar, but the bass and the keys as well. He went on to play for a few bands in high school, notably X-Tassy.
Influenced by his favorite producers, Timbaland and Pharell Williams, RichArt MusiQ grew an interest in music production. He has been making beats since Fruity Loops 3.56. At the time, he was one of the few people in Haiti to make beats on a DAW. He used to bring his laptop to school to show his craft. Throughout the years, RichArt MusiQ taught and mentored a few other artists and producers.
Over the years, RichArt MusiQ perfected his craft by not following trends but by focusing on sounding different. His preferred writing subject is what he refers to as, "musical porn", erotism through rap.
In the past few years, RichArt MusiQ has grown into a Mixing Engineer and has been working with different artists worldwide.
